Laparoscopy

Laparoscopy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure used in the field of gynecology to diagnose and treat various conditions affecting the female reproductive system. At Nirmala Hospital, laparoscopy is performed by experienced gynecologists to address issues such as ovarian cysts, endometriosis, fibroids, and other pelvic conditions with minimal discomfort and a quicker recovery time compared to traditional surgery.

Indications for Laparoscopy in Gynecology

  • Have pre-existing medical conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, or autoimmune disorders
  • Are carrying multiple fetuses (twins, triplets, etc.)
  • Are of advanced maternal age (35 years or older)
  • Have a history of pregnancy complications, such as preterm labor, preeclampsia, or miscarriage
  • Develop complications during pregnancy, such as gestational diabetes, placenta previa, or fetal growth restrictions

Benefits of Laparoscopy

Specialized Monitoring

Patients receive frequent and detailed monitoring, allowing for early detection and management of potential complications.

Customized Care

The care plan is tailored to the specific risks and needs of the mother and baby, ensuring that both receive the best possible care.

Emotional Support

High-risk pregnancies can be stressful, and specialized care includes support from healthcare providers who understand the emotional challenges involved.
