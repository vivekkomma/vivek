About Dr K. Nirmala

Dr. K. Nirmala is the Founder and Medical Director of Nirmala Hospital, bringing over 25 years of specialized experience in High Risk Obstetrics and Gynecology. Her extensive career is marked by a profound dedication to advancing women’s health through innovative practices and compassionate care. Dr. Nirmala’s expertise in handling complex and high-risk pregnancies has earned her a distinguished reputation in the medical community.

Her areas of specialization include High Risk Obstetrics and Gynecology, Reproductive Endocrinology, Infertility, and Gynecology. Dr. Nirmala is also skilled in advanced procedures such as Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy, which enable her to provide comprehensive and effective treatment options for her patients. Her commitment to excellence and patient-centered approach ensures that every individual receives the highest standard of care.
